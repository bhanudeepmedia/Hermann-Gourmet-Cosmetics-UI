We Cook Cosmetics.

A story of heritage, mixing, and love.

It started in a kitchen.

Hermann Gourmet Cosmetics wasn't born in a laboratory. It was born in the foothills of the Troodos mountains, in a kitchen filled with the scent of dried roses and beeswax.

Our founder, inspired by her grandmother's handwritten recipe book, realized that modern skincare had lost its soul. It was sterile, mass-produced, and full of fillers.
